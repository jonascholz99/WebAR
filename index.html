WebAR Examples
Hiro Kanji A B GitHub
This webpage is a comprehensive resource for exploring WebAR, featuring an array of tests that delve into the vast potential of Augmented Reality on the web. Each example on the page uniquely incorporates different technologies, showcasing the versatility and wide-ranging applicability of WebAR.

ARjs & A-Frame - Image Tracking
This example is an implementation of image tracking and the display of a GLTF model with AR.js and A-Frame.
ARjs & ThreeJS - Marker Tracking
In this example, marker tracking is used with AR.js and Three.js. A simple cube is displayed on the marker.
ARjs & ThreeJS - 3D Model
Here, a 3D object is also displayed, but not through image tracking. Instead, marker tracking with AR.js and Three.js is used.
ARjs & ThreeJS - AR Portal
On the marker, a portal appears. You can look around it with the camera and see a different place through the portal.
ARjs & ThreeJS - Shadow
In this example, you can see how shadows can also be displayed. Here, objects cast dynamic shadows even when moving.
ARjs & ThreeJS - Multible Markers
In this example, it is shown how objects can be displayed on multiple markers simultaneously.
